About

In my studio practice I am inspired by culture, identity, audience and their intersection. Through bold authorship, deep understanding and ambitious goal setting, I always aim to make engaging, visually and intellectually interesting work that inspires. ​

Sarah Cooper Lucerto is a graphic designer based in Boston, MA. She has a BA in International Relations from Boston University and is currently a Graphic Design MFA candidate, also at Boston University. Her 10 years of experience in international education management has taken her around the world, and put her face-to-face with some super inspiring people, young and old. Her travels also inspired her to dig deeper into her design practice. When she isn’t designing (which is a lot of the time), she likes to travel, try new restaurants, frequent local watering holes, and is a voracious podcast listener.
